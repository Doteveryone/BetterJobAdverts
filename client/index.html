About this site

Having lots of jobs on one website, and making it easy to search them is really important. So are alerts, to you can get notified on your phone when there is a new job.

Unfortunately, employers and jobs sites publish very low quality job adverts. Salaries, geographical location, skills needed are unclear and inconsistent.

There is an international standard (called schema.org JobPosting) for publishing job adverts with proper data. This site can check a jobs website to see if it is using that standard.
