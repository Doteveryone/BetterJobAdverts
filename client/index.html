About this site

Job hunters want great services to find jobs - ones that list every job there is, with accurate up to date details, and alerts when new ones appear.

Unfortunately, this isn't possible because employers and job sites publish very low quality job adverts. Salaries, geographical location, skills needed are unclear and inconsistent. They can't be easily reused by other sites and apps.

There is an international standard (called schema.org JobPosting) for publishing job adverts with proper data. Open Jobs checks a jobs website to see if it is using that standard. We award Gold, Silver and Bronze grades to sites which pass.
